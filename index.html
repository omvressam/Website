About us





History

Porsche, founded by Ferdinand Porsche in 1931, initially offered consulting and development work for other automotive companies. Read More

In 1948, the company introduced its first production car, the Porsche 356, which quickly gained acclaim for its performance and design.

Mission

Porsche's mission is to create and deliver high-performance sports cars, SUVs, and sedans that provide unparalleled driving experiences. Read More

They aim to combine innovation, precision engineering, and iconic design to exceed customer expectations.

Notable Achievements

  • Introducing the iconic Porsche 911 in 1963, which has become one of the most recognizable sports cars in history. Read More
  • Winning numerous motorsport championships, including the 24 Hours of Le Mans, Formula One, and the World Endurance Championship.
  • Pioneering advancements in automotive technology, such as the development of hybrid and electric vehicles like the Porsche Taycan.
  • Consistently ranking among the top luxury automotive brands for quality, performance, and customer satisfaction.
